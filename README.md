# LucraGrace - Profit Calculator

LucraGrace is a web-based profit margin and pricing calculator designed for ecommerce, retail, and small business owners. Quickly calculate optimal selling prices by entering your costs, setting your desired profit margin, and seeing recommended prices in real time.

## Features
- Calculate product cost, shipping, ad spend, and transaction fees.
- Set your desired profit margin with an interactive slider.
- Get instant recommended selling price and profit per unit.
- Copy price to clipboard for easy use.
- Sleek, modern, and mobile-friendly design.
- Completely free to use.

## Built With
- HTML5, CSS3 (TailwindCSS)
- JavaScript
- Google Fonts (Manrope & Inter)
- Material Symbols Outlined icons
